ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Khristian Thompson UX/UI designer, programmer, and cyber operations technician. You may be thinking wow that's broad and it most definitely is, but I owe it all to my experiences and job status within the Air Force. I am from a small town in Alabama where one day I decided I wanted to see the world and joined the military where I found myself surrounded by information technology. The endless training and broad spectrum experiences opened my eyes to the avenues within this field where I adopted my passion for I.T.

One day, an opportunity came across my email to partake in a UX/UI bootcamp at the University of Utah at the militaries expense. I jumped on that opportunity without hesitation and spent the next 6 months developing an understanding of UX/UI concepts where I then became an abled Jr. UX/UI designer. Four projects later and some experience in different facets I have now developed a great understanding of the many pillars of UX/UI.
